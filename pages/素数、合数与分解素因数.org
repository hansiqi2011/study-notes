** n的正约数的和为：$(p_1^0+p_1^1+p_1^2+ \dots + p_1^{a_1})(p_2^0+p_2^1+p_2^2+ \dots + p_x^{a_2}) \dots (p_k^0+p_k^1+p_k^2+ \dots + p_k^{a_k})$
